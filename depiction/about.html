About Papercuts

Papercuts is a standard format for Shortcut repositories, ensuring interoperability between clients like Davinci or Equestria. Think like Cydia Sources, but for Shortcuts.

Clients

  • The official web client
  • Davinci (unreleased)
  • Equestria
